About Us

What’s up! I’m a 13 year old boy from Munich, and I’ve launched my own clothing brand from scratch.

What makes it different? All my designs are based on cities. Every shirt, hoodie, or piece I drop is inspired by the vibe, energy, and culture of a specific place. From the skyline to the street art, I turn the soul of each city into something you can wear.

My mission? I want to create a design for every city in the world. Big or small — they all matter. Everyone deserves to rep where they’re from or where they feel at home.

So whether you’re from New York, Tokyo, London, or Munich— I want you to be able to say: “Yeah, that’s my city. And I’m wearing it.”

Let’s build this together. One city at a time.
